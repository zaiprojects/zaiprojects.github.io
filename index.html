Introduction

Contents
 This website contains sections of multimedia content paired with explanations of their significance.

These “artifacts” are intended to communicate my personal worldview to the viewer through more than words alone.

As a secondary goal, I hope to tell a pseudostory throughout the website, so the reader not only understands my worldview, but experiences some of the emotions that I have experienced.

WARNING: This website contains flashing lights and occasional disturbing language.

Artifacts
 The layout of my website is fairly normal.

Each artifact is a seperate webpage, and each one will contain a link to the next.

The order of the artifacts is based on their level of abstraction.

I would highly encourage that the artifacts be observed in this order for the sake of both comprehension and immersion.

I intend to be very deliberate with their placement.

Additionally, I do not recommend doing a full walkthrough of the website before reading, as I will be using the progress through the webpage to possibly trigger some artistic effects that will be best saved for the right moment in the readings.

Guidance and Acknowldegement
 I understand that this whole concept is a bit self-indulgent, and the reader may, at times, disagree with my sentiments or become confused with my logic.

While I will do my best to express my thoughts effectively, I ask you to read this webpage with either an open mind, or a curiosity to learn.
As a final thought, please understand that the communication style in each level of this website changes.

The thoughts and sentences will become more abstract as the experience progresses.
